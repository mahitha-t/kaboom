🃏 Cambio

A card game of memory and strategy

or

How to Play

  • Each player gets 4 face-down cards. You peek at your bottom 2 at the start.
  • Goal: have the lowest total score when someone calls Cambio.
  • On your turn: draw from the deck, then swap it with one of your cards or discard it.
  • Sticking: When a card is discarded, if you have a matching value you can "stick" it onto the pile. You can also stick an opponent's card and give them one of yours.
  • Special cards:
    • 7/8 — Peek at one of your own cards
    • 9/10 — Peek at one of an opponent's cards
    • Jack — Blind swap (your card ↔ opponent's)
    • Queen — Peek at any card, then optionally swap it with any other
    • Black King — Peek at any 2 cards, then optionally swap them
  • Scoring: A=1, 2-10=face value, J=11, Q=12, Black K=25, Red K=−1, Joker=−1
  • Cambio! Call it when you think you're lowest. Everyone else gets one more turn, then reveal.
